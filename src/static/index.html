About LightMap Boston

Find shade by day. Find light by night.

An interactive map of Boston and Cambridge showing shadow coverage during the day and streetlight brightness at night.

Data Sources

  • Buildings: 46,386 (Boston BPDA + Cambridge GIS). Height from 2010/2018 surveys.
  • Tree canopy: 138,099 polygons. Boston 2019, Cambridge 2018.
  • Streetlights: 80,182 (Boston + Cambridge city-owned).
  • Crime: 257K incidents (Boston 2023-2026, Cambridge 2009-2026).
  • Flood risk: 5,705 complaints from 311 (2019-2024).
  • Ice risk: 42,275 complaints from 311 (2019-2024).
  • Weather: Real-time from Open-Meteo API.

Limitations

  • Building heights are from 2010 (Boston) / 2018 (Cambridge). New construction is not reflected.
  • Tree canopy data is from 2018-2019. Trees may have been added or removed.
  • Flood/ice risk is based on historical complaints, not real-time conditions.
  • Shadow computation uses simplified geometry. Actual shadows may differ slightly.

All data from data.boston.gov and data.cambridgema.gov. Downloaded 2026-04-01.
